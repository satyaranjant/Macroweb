Civil & Labour License

In a strategic move to mitigate issues related to tender manipulation and usher in a new era of transparency in the bid submission process, the government has taken a resolute step towards the complete digitization of the e-procurement process. Currently, within the framework of every tender, there are two essential components that necessitate manual submission by bidders to designated officers:

1. Earnest Money Deposit (EMD): Presently, this critical financial guarantee is presented in physical forms, typically as Demand Drafts or Bank Guarantees.

2. Labor License: This imperative document, the labor license, stands as a prerequisite for specific businesses and organizations seeking to engage in legal labor hiring practices.

At our organization, we are dedicated to providing expert consultancy services in the domains of civil licenses and labor licenses. We serve as a legal beacon, guiding and facilitating various contractors in their pursuit of compliance with these essential requisites.

GST & Income Tax

In India, businesses with an annual turnover exceeding INR 40 lakhs are required to obtain a Goods and Services Tax (GST) number. However, there is a potential upcoming change that may allow businesses with turnover below this threshold to engage in online selling without the need for GST registration.

Furthermore, the Income Tax department has introduced the "e-proceeding" facility, revolutionizing the process of conducting tax proceedings electronically. This innovation facilitates a seamless exchange of letters, notices, communications, questionnaires, orders, and more between the Income Tax department and taxpayers, all without the need for taxpayers to physically visit an income tax office.

Our organization stands at the forefront, providing expert consultancy services in the realms of GST and Income Tax. We cater to a diverse clientele, assisting both individuals and organizations in navigating the complexities of these tax domains.
