About Macroweb Technology

INTRODUCTION

Since its establishment in 1995, MACROWEB TECHNOLOGY has continually evolved into an innovative, unique, and industry-relevant IT training provider. The past decade has witnessed a profound transformation in the IT industry, where numerous international brands have struggled to reach rural markets, thereby limiting the potential of countless talented individuals in these regions. With a comprehensive approach, a wide spectrum of programs, pedagogical excellence, high-quality instruction, career guidance, and need-oriented counseling, this educational institution is dedicated to enhancing IT skills.

VISION

Our primary goal is to function as a leading finishing school for rural talents in the IT sector. We are unwavering in our commitment to empowering our trainees with the necessary skills and knowledge, equipping them to excel in the dynamic and competitive IT industry. Our unique approach combines personalized education, practical experience, and a strong focus on soft skills development. We foster an inclusive and diverse learning environment, ensuring equal opportunities for all, and actively engage with the local community to create a positive impact.

MISSION

Our dedicated mission is to set a benchmark for excellence in Education & Training with MACROWEB TECHNOLOGY, striving to produce highly acclaimed IT professionals. We are deeply committed to providing abundant opportunities for rural talents and delivering top-notch education at an affordable cost. This empowers them to harness their full potential and contribute significantly to the nation's growth, making a meaningful impact in whichever sphere they choose to pursue.

INDUSTRY

India has indisputably ascended to prominence as a vital global IT destination. The convergence of human talent, industrial expertise, and cutting-edge technology has catalyzed its extraordinary growth. The demand for Indian software professionals can be attributed to their intellectual prowess, proficiency in the English language, and adeptness in IT. Furthermore, advanced programs are readily accessible at more economical rates in smaller townships, eliminating the need for extensive travel in pursuit of specialized courses. This inclusivity underscores the widespread reach of IT education, extending to every corner of the country. The landscape is adorned with software parks, E-Governance initiatives, E-Panchayats, IT kiosks, and educational opportunities for children. These initiatives not only speak volumes but also serve as self-explanatory testaments to India's IT prowess. In the words of the insightful Alvin Toffler, "The illiterate of the 21st century will not be those who cannot read and write, but those who cannot learn, unlearn, and relearn."

Our Mantra

Our generation has borne witness to the ever-changing tides of time. History has unfolded and been rewritten, with world wars, nuclear treaties, moon landings, and the profound advancement of scientific theories such as black holes. In the midst of these wonders, one invention stands out as a transformative force that has impacted every facet of our lives: the computer. It has turned our world into a global village, revolutionizing the way we connect, learn, and work. Since the 1980s, numerous multinational corporations (MNCs) have sprung up and claimed to have trained millions, but strangely, very few have reached the remote corners of rural India. We, however, are determined to bridge this gap and tap into the immense talent and potential that lies within these rural areas. We have not only witnessed the tripling of the world economy and the rare occurrence of recessions but have also observed organizations emphasizing cost control while seeking individuals who are not just willing to contribute but are also equipped with a versatile skill set. Macroweb Technology, established in 1995, has stood the test of time, growing and evolving as the years have passed. Like the old saying goes, "He who strives never perishes." We continue to flourish as we adapt to the changing landscape of the IT industry. Imagine, for a moment, if there were a university in place of the Konark Temple, where 1200 professors had dedicated 12 years to impart knowledge. We might well be living in a different civilization today. This underscores the irreplaceable value of education. In the midst of the ongoing IT revolution, we believe it's essential for all of us to seize the opportunities it presents. If we cannot directly cross the river, we should, at the very least, learn to swim in the direction it flows. Rest assured, we are committed to providing our best services to support your journey towards becoming a distinguished IT professional. Embrace the potential and possibilities of the IT world with us.
