About the Macroweb

Since inception in 1995 Macroweb Technology has grown into an innovative, unique and industry relevant training and Service Provider in IT, Telecom, Electronics and allied Vocational Sector. The last decade has seen many changes so-called Micro-revolutionary in various spheres.

Though many IT majors have tall claims of causing IT literacy movement in India, much more is left yet to be reached out millions of rural talents are deprived of vocational and skill oriented training. Allured by spurious organizations many have shattered their dreams. Most Govt. Projects, People Centric Schemes measurably failed in meeting national objective.

Since 1995, MACROWEB TECHNOLOGY has evolved into an innovative IT training pioneer. The company has consistently led in the ever-evolving IT landscape.

We strive to enhance the skills of rural talents in IT as a finishing school so that our satisfied trainees can be made ready to contribute for industry by being employed or self employed.

We sincerely strive to make MACROWEB TECHNOLOGY a model organization in Education & Training to produce acclaimed IT professionals.

India has already emerged as an important IT destination, globally. The amalgamation of resources like human, industry & technology has caused phenomenal growth.

Our Mantra

The world is filled with extraordinary achievements, from conquering the moon to delving into the mysteries of black holes. Yet, among these remarkable wonders, the invention of the computer stands as a beacon that has forever altered the course of human existence. It has transformed our planet into a global e-village, connecting people from all corners of the Earth. However, despite the proliferation of so-called multinational corporations (MNCs) since the 1980s, it's remarkable that very few have reached the remote heartlands of rural India.

We, on the other hand, embrace the challenge of nurturing the latent talents within these rural communities and turning them into accomplished performers. In doing so, we have witnessed the exponential growth of the global economy, with rare instances of recession. Many forward-thinking organizations, in their pursuit of cost control, are now seeking skilled and adaptable professionals who are ready to make a significant impact.

Our journey is one of continuous growth, and we dare to dream of what might have been if a university had stood in place of the majestic Konark Temple, with 1,200 dedicated professors laboring for 12 years to shape a different civilization. It serves as a powerful reminder that nothing can truly replace the importance of education. In times of IT revolution, it is imperative for all of us to seize this opportunity. Even if we cannot completely traverse the river, we should, at the very least, learn to swim in the direction it flows.

We pledge our unwavering commitment to provide the finest services to support your journey in becoming a renowned IT professional. Your future endeavors hold great promise, and we are here to help you reach your aspirations.
